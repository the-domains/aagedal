About me

Norwegian film director.

Worked at NRK Lillehammer from 2011–2015 while studying Film and Television science, completing a bachelor degree in 2013.

Studied documentary film production at Lillehammer University College from 2013 to 2016.

Films

The world as we know it changes every second by people whom dare to imagine the future.
My work as a photographer began with a film about living together in harmony.
I continued then to direct and photograph a film about the fact that it is sometimes better to die sooner rather than later.
But to finalise my studies in documentary filmmaking, I wanted to explore a question I had struggled with for a long time: What is the meaning of life?

– directing the world towards the future –
